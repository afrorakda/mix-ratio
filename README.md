# MIX RATIO | Fridge Combo #112

A hyper-practical dilution calculator designed for household and professional use. It removes the mental math of "1 part to X parts" and ensures you never overflow your container or damage surfaces with incorrect concentrations.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Instant dilution calculation without the math headache.
- **Fail-Safe Logic**: Automatically calculates based on "1 part concentrate + X parts water," the global standard for cleaners and fertilizers.
- **Real-World Ready**: Results are rounded to practical measurements (integers for ml/oz, and fractions for cups).

## Features
- **Smart Rounding**: 
    - **ml / oz**: Rounded to integers for easy measuring.
    - **gal**: Rounded to 1 decimal place.
    - **cups**: Automatically rounded to the nearest **1/4, 1/2, or 3/4** to match standard measuring cups.
- **Safety First**: Clearly reminds you to add water first to prevent excessive foaming.
- **Multi-Unit Support**: Toggle between ml, oz, gal, and cups instantly. The unit choice is for your comfort—the ratio remains perfect.
- **Fast Input**: Mobile-optimized with "Next" key support to jump between fields.

## Usage
1. Select your preferred unit (e.g., ml or cups).
2. Enter the **Total Volume** you want to make (your bottle or bucket size).
3. Enter the **Ratio (X)** found on the product label (e.g., for 1:10, just enter 10).
4. Follow the "Water to add" and "Concentrate" results.

## Privacy & Performance
- **No Login**: Use it the moment you need to mix.
- **No Tracking**: Your measurements stay in your browser.
- **No Ads**: Clean, focused interface for busy hands.
- **Browser Only**: Lightweight and responsive on any mobile device.
